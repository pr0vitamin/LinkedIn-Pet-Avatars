# Privacy Policy

LinkedIn Pet Avatars stores only the data required to run the extension on your device.

## What the extension stores

- Your on/off preference for the extension
- Pet photos that you import through the extension UI
- Basic metadata for those imported photos, such as image dimensions and upload time
- Limited LinkedIn page content needed to identify which visible images are likely profile avatars

## Where data is stored

- Imported photos are stored locally inside the extension on your device
- Avatar-selection logic runs locally in the browser on LinkedIn pages
- The extension does not upload your photos to any server
- The extension does not sync your imported photos to other devices in this version

## Network access

- The extension runs on `linkedin.com` pages so it can replace visible profile images
- The extension does not upload your photos to our servers or a third-party API
- When enabled, the extension displays your chosen photos inside the LinkedIn pages you visit, so those images are intentionally rendered on `linkedin.com`
